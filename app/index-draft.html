Future

Proof

Electrica – a rapidly growing company at the heart of the rapidly-building revolution that encompasses clean green electric transportation, sports, leisure, infrastructure and so much more.

Immersive, educational, consumer-centred events

Our city-centre and public facing events place the consumer at the centre of all that will fashion Europe’s electrification programme – from sustainable green energy provision, and home harvesting, to storage and vehicle charging tech along, the whole new world of cheap, clean, electric powered vehicles and the products that will excite and play an increasingly larger part in our leisure and entertainment.
We bring all the latest electric technology and its applications together, allowing the consumer to look, see, touch, feel and often test the future.

We do it in an interesting and educational way, via immersive displays and live demonstrations that connect members of the public with the constantly evolving and many faceted worlds of electronic transportation, sport and leisure.

Shaping the Future

Our world is changing faster than ever before, and Electrica is at the forefront of delivering events and infrastructure that allow all of us to discover what the future holds. There are so many questions to be answered about electric mobility.

Electrica provides the medium by which perceptions can be changed, new technology embraced, and our eyes opened to the opportunities of an environmentally friendly world where our everyday lives, business and pleasure will be vastly improved by the flick of a switch.
